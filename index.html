I am a postdoctoral fellow at the Department of Biosystems Science and Engineering, ETH Zurich. I am also an affiliated researcher at the ETH AI Center and the SIB Swiss Institute For Bioinformatics

Before I came to ETH, I had the pleasure to work with Laust Hvas Mortensen, Søren Brunak and several other excellent researchers at Denmark Statistik and the University of Copenhagen. During my Ph.D. at Cambridge University and EMBL-EBI, I worked with Moritz Gerstung and Ewan Birney. I received my MSc. in Statistics from Edinburgh University and my BSc. in Economics from University of Bamberg.

A current CV is available here.

My research interest center around survival and event history analysis with a particular focus on method development and applications to electronic health records (EHR) More recently, I have been working on generative AI and causal inference - you may find a more detailed expose in Research.

If you are interest to discuss or collaborate on a problem in this direction, don’t hesitate to contact me by email.
